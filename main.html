Brand Alignment (×15)

  • Deep understanding of our brand, culture & values; similar clients; great chemistry; “gets it” immediately

  • Basic understanding, generic approach, willing to learn

Brand Strategy (×10)

  • Full strategic capabilities, research-driven, positioning experts
  • Good strategy integration with design
  • Basic strategy, design-focused
  • Execution only, minimal strategy

Logo Design Expertise (×10)

  • Specialized logo expertise, iconic work in portfolio, multiple rebrands
  • Strong logo design capabilities, good examples
  • Competent logo work, limited rebrand experience
  • Generic or template-based approach

Core Messaging Experience (×10)

  • Dedicated copywriters/strategists, messaging frameworks, verbal identity systems
  • In-house messaging capabilities, good written work
  • Partner with external copywriters, basic messaging
  • Design-only focus, outsource all copy

Team Diversity (×5)

  • Highly diverse leadership & team (gender, race, background, thought), inclusive culture
  • Good diversity representation, active DEI initiatives
  • Some diversity, awareness of importance
  • Homogeneous team, no visible diversity efforts
